Budget

The Antenna Test Range was given $1095 at the beginning of the project to purchase a linear guide rail system. After a proposal to Keysight, our team was given a generous donation of $500 to supplement our project. The project encountered numerous problems with the RF coaxial cables and a generous donation of 35' SMA cables and adapters was supplied by LGS Innovations. The project was able to complete our design specifications with a leftover budget of $187. Future teams can implement changes to the rotary joints and purchase an LNA to increase the accuracy of far-field measurements for any antenna.
